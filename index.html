I am currently a senior researcher in AI Center Toronto, Samsung Electronics. Before that, I obtained my PhD in Computing Science from the University of Alberta, where I was advised by Professor Herb Yang. My research interests span computer vision, with a focus on AIGC, including text-guided image, video, and 3D generation/editing, as well as multi-modal learning. I have also worked on computational photography challenges, such as super-resolution, image deblurring, and spectral reflectance recovery.
